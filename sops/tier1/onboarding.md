## 1. Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes a consistent process for onboarding new employees to ensure a positive experience and compliance with company requirements.

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to all full-time, part-time, and contract employees.

## 3. Responsible Parties

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| HR Coordinator | Manage onboarding process |
| Hiring Manager | Welcome and orient new hire |
| IT Department | Equipment and access setup |
| Department Buddy | First-week support |

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Pre-Start (1-2 weeks before)

- [ ] Send welcome email with start details
- [ ] Complete I-9 and tax forms
- [ ] Set up workstation and equipment
- [ ] Create email and system accounts
- [ ] Assign parking/building access
- [ ] Notify team of new hire arrival

### 4.2 First Day

| Time | Activity | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9:00 AM | Welcome meeting | HR |
| 9:30 AM | IT setup and training | IT |
| 10:30 AM | Office tour | Manager |
| 11:00 AM | Benefits enrollment | HR |
| 12:00 PM | Team lunch | Team |
| 1:00 PM | Policy review | HR |
| 2:00 PM | Role-specific training | Manager |
| 4:00 PM | Check-in with buddy | Buddy |

### 4.3 First Week

- [ ] Complete all required training modules
- [ ] Review employee handbook
- [ ] Meet with manager for 30/60/90 day goals
- [ ] Set up one-on-ones with team members
- [ ] Complete equipment inventory sign-off

### 4.4 First 30 Days

- [ ] Complete all compliance training
- [ ] First project assignment
- [ ] 30-day check-in with HR
- [ ] Feedback session with manager
