## 1. Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure establishes a consistent process for onboarding new employees to ensure a positive experience and compliance with company requirements.

---

## 2. Scope

This procedure applies to:
- Full-time employees
- Part-time employees
- Contract employees
- Interns

---

## 3. Responsible Parties

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| HR Coordinator | Manage onboarding process |
| Hiring Manager | Welcome and orient new hire |
| IT Department | Equipment and access setup |
| Department Buddy | First-week support |

---

## 4. Procedure

### 4.1 Pre-Start (1-2 weeks before)

- [ ] Send welcome email with start details
- [ ] Complete I-9 and tax forms
- [ ] Set up workstation and equipment
- [ ] Create email and system accounts
- [ ] Assign parking/building access
- [ ] Notify team of new hire arrival
- [ ] Prepare onboarding packet
- [ ] Schedule first-week meetings

### 4.2 First Day

| Time | Activity | Owner |
|------|----------|-------|
| 9:00 AM | Welcome & paperwork | HR |
| 10:00 AM | IT setup & training | IT |
| 12:00 PM | Team lunch | Manager |
| 1:00 PM | Workspace tour | Buddy |
| 2:00 PM | Policy overview | HR |
| 3:00 PM | Meet key stakeholders | Manager |
| 4:00 PM | First-week schedule review | Manager |

### 4.3 First Week

- [ ] Complete all required training
- [ ] Meet with direct reports (if manager)
- [ ] Set 30-60-90 day goals
- [ ] Review team structure and workflows
- [ ] Schedule ongoing check-ins

### 4.4 First 30 Days

- [ ] Complete onboarding checklist
- [ ] Submit benefits enrollment
- [ ] Attend department meetings
- [ ] Complete project assignments
- [ ] 30-day check-in with manager
