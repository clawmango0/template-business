## Service Agreement Prompt

```
Based on our company:
- Industry: [YOUR INDUSTRY]
- Location: [CITY, STATE]
- Company Size: [NUMBER] employees
- Services: [LIST YOUR SERVICES]

Create a professional service agreement that includes:
- Service description for [YOUR SERVICE]
- Payment terms
- Liability limitations
- Cancellation policy
- Insurance details

Make it appropriate for [RESIDENTIAL/COMMERCIAL] clients in [YOUR STATE].
```

