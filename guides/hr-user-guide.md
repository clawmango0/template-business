# User Guide: HR Templates

## Overview

HR templates help standardize people operations - from hiring to performance to offboarding. This guide covers how to use each template effectively.

---

## Template Overview

| Template | When to Use | HR Phase |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| Employee Handbook | Company scaling, first hires | Onboarding |
| Job Description | New role, hiring | Recruiting |
| Performance Review | Annual/bi-annual reviews | Development |

---

## How to Use Each Template

### 1. Employee Handbook

**Purpose:** Set expectations, policies, and culture for all employees.

**When to create:**
- Company reaches 10+ employees
- First round of hiring
- Legal compliance needs

**Sections to customize:**
- [ ] Company mission/values
- [ ] Employment policies
- [ ] Compensation & benefits
- [ ] Workplace policies
- [ ] Code of conduct
- [ ] Acknowledgment section

**Best practices:**
- Have employment lawyer review
- Update annually
- Require signed acknowledgment

### 2. Job Description

**Purpose:** Clearly define role expectations for candidates and hiring managers.

**When to create:**
- New role
- Role significantly changes
- Hiring for open position

**Key sections:**
- Position overview
- Responsibilities (core + daily)
- Required qualifications
- Preferred qualifications
- Compensation & benefits
- Application instructions

**Tips:**
- Be specific - avoid jargon
- Include must-haves vs nice-to-haves
- Be realistic about requirements

### 3. Performance Review

**Purpose:** Formal assessment of employee performance and growth.

**When to use:**
- Annual reviews
- Mid-year check-ins
- Probation reviews
- 360 reviews

**Process:**
1. Employee completes self-assessment
2. Manager completes evaluation
3. HR reviews for consistency
4. Live discussion
5. Goal setting for next period

**Rating scale:**
| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 5 | Exceptional - Role model |
| 4 | Exceeds expectations |
| 3 | Meets expectations |
| 2 | Developing |
| 1 | Needs improvement |

---

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Generic job descriptions | Be specific to your company |
| No handbook | Create early, update often |
| Rare reviews | Schedule quarterly check-ins |
| No development plans | Add in every review |
| Vague feedback | Use specific examples |

---

## Legal Considerations

### Must-Have Policies
- Equal opportunity statement
- At-will employment (US)
- Harassment policy
- Safety policies

### Recommended
- Remote work policy
- Social media policy
- Confidentiality agreement reference
- Device usage policy

### Have Lawyer Review
- Termination procedures
- Compensation structures
- Benefits descriptions
