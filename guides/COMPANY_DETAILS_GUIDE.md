## What to Include (Priority Order)

### Must Have (5 min)
1. Company Name
2. Industry
3. Location (affects employment law)
4. Company Size (affects HR policies)

### Should Have (10 min)
5. Revenue stage (affects financial docs)
6. Target customer (affects marketing)
7. Key values (affects culture docs)

### Nice to Have (15 min)
8. Tools you use
9. Goals
10. Competitors

## Example Workflow

### Creating a Job Description

1. **Open** COMPANY.md
2. **Copy** relevant sections:
   - Industry: Construction
   - Size: 15 employees
   - Location: Fort Worth, TX
   - Benefits: Health, 401K, PTO
   - Culture: Family-oriented, casual

3. **Paste** into AI with prompt:
```
Based on our company:
- Industry: Construction (commercial & residential)
- Size: 15 employees, 3 contractors
- Location: Fort Worth, TX
- Benefits: Health insurance, 401K match, unlimited PTO
- Culture: Family-oriented, casual

Create a job description for a Project Manager that fits our culture and includes:
- Typical responsibilities for construction PM
- Requirements matching our size
- Benefits we offer
- How to apply
```

4. **Get** personalized job description!
