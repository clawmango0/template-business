## CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**Disclosing Party:** {{COMPANY_NAME}} ("Company")
**Receiving Party:** [RECEIVING PARTY NAME] ("Recipient")

## 1. PURPOSE

The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship ("Purpose") and in connection with the Purpose, the Disclosing Party may disclose to the Receiving Party certain confidential and proprietary information.

## 2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any information disclosed by Company to Recipient, either directly or indirectly, in writing, orally, or by inspection of tangible objects, that is designated as "Confidential," "Proprietary," or some similar designation.

### Includes but is not limited to:
- Business plans and strategies
- Financial information
- Customer lists and data
- Pricing information
- Technical data and specifications
- Trade secrets
- Product roadmaps

## 3. EXCLUSIONS

Confidential Information does NOT include information that:
- Was publicly known at the time of disclosure
- Becomes publicly known through no fault of Recipient
- Was rightfully in Recipient's possession prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed by Recipient
- Is disclosed with Company's prior written approval

## 4. OBLIGATIONS

Recipient agrees to:
- Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Not disclose to any third parties without prior written consent
- Use only for the Purpose
- Protect with at least the same degree of care used for own confidential information
- Limit access to need-to-know employees
- Not copy or reproduce without permission

## 5. TERM

This Agreement shall remain in effect for {{YEARS}} years from the date of disclosure.

Obligations regarding trade secrets shall continue for as long as such information remains trade secrets.

## 6. RETURN OF INFORMATION

Upon request or termination of discussions, Recipient shall promptly:
- Return all Confidential Information
- Destroy all copies and notes
- Certify destruction in writing

## 7. OWNERSHIP

All Confidential Information remains the sole property of Company. No license or rights are granted except as specifically stated.

## 8. NO OBLIGATION

Nothing in this Agreement obligates either party to proceed with any transaction or relationship.

## 9. REMEDIES

Recipient acknowledges that breach may cause irreparable harm. Company shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunction and specific performance, in addition to other remedies.

## 10. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of {{STATE}}.

## 11. ENTIRE AGREEMENT

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties concerning the subject matter hereof.
